What does the bot really do?

  • ShitBot DEXtrader V5 uses a hybrid trailing grid system with dynamic allocation based on portfolio value.
  • You can choose either DCA-grid strategy, or Asset-balancer.
  • DCA-grid will focus on holding USDT, split the allocation for 4-grid orders. Ratio of lots:DCA distance is 1,1,2,3 : 0,-3%,-7%,-15%. TP trailing will be triggered at +0.9% with 0.2% callback rates to lock the profit. It uses AI-module to get the BUY signal for opening new position.
  • Asset-balancer strategy is straightforwad, will focus to hold USDT and WMATIC at 50:50 ratio. Any value changes of 5% on each side will trigger either buy more WMATIC or sell it to USDT.
